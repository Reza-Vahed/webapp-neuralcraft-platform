## Your role

As an AI Engineer at NeuralCraft, you'll work alongside our team to build custom AI agents, automations, and AI-powered applications for clients across industries and company sizes. You're not just an engineer — you're a technical sparring partner for our clients.

## What to expect

- Direct collaboration with clients — from requirements analysis to go-live
- Varied projects: from chatbots to workflow automation to custom AI agents
- A team that values clean code, honest communication, and measurable outcomes
- Remote-first, with occasional on-site client visits

## How to apply

Send us a short message with your CV and, if you have them, examples of past projects, to careers@neuralcraft.ai.
