## Starting point

A regional electrical trade business with 18 employees received a high volume of appointment requests across multiple channels every day. Coordination ran entirely manually through the office: answer the call, check capacity in a paper or spreadsheet plan, call back. During sick leave or high demand, double-bookings or missed callbacks were common.

## Approach

We built a chat assistant available on the website and via WhatsApp. The assistant:

- captures the type of request (e.g. repair, new installation, maintenance) and its urgency,
- checks open slots against the technicians' existing capacity plan,
- suggests matching time windows and confirms the appointment automatically,
- escalates urgent or unclear cases directly to the office.

The existing planning logic in the office was deliberately not replaced, but connected via an interface — keeping the core workflow familiar for the team.

## Result

Within the first month, average phone time per employee dropped by three hours per week. Customers can now request appointments outside office hours and typically receive confirmation within minutes. Double-bookings have all but disappeared thanks to the automated capacity check.
